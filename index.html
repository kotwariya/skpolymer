Our Products

We Provide the one of the refined quality in the following product

Guar Seeds

The Guar or cluster bean, with the botanical name Cyamopsis tetragonoloba, is an annual legume and the source of guar gum. It is also known as Gavar, Guwar, or Guvar bean.

Guar Korma

The processed Guar Korma Meal is usually rich in proteins and carbohydrates and forms a high protein food for animals, cattle and poultry. Guar Korma Meal is mainly used to feed the milk giving animals to enhance their milk giving capacity.

Guar Churi

Guar meal (churi) is having upto 43-45% protein contents, Guar meal (churi) comes as husk/Powder form. Guar meal (Churi) is 100% natural agricultural product without addition of any chemicals or preservatives.

Guar Gum Split Refined

Guar Gum Refined Split is refined endosperm derived from guar seed or cluster bean (cyamoperm tetragonoloba). It is a non-ionic polysaccharide galacomannan.
